Technology and Apps


This course will rely heavily on digital mapping technology. All software required for this course will be freely accessible, or accessible at the campus computer lab. You will need access to a laptop or desktop computer (your own or at the computer lab) for specific mapping applications, while in other cases a tablet computer should be sufficient. ms of hardware, for most exercises, a tablet computer should be sufficient. Specific programs will be using include:

  • Leaflet maps: https://leafletjs.com/
  • Google apps: creating a Google account will give you access to many of the applications we will use this semester
  • QGIS: https://qgis.org/de/site/ (reqiures software installation on laptop or desktop)
  • Open Street Maps: https://www.openstreetmap.org/ (create a free online account)
  • Mapbox: https://www.mapbox.com/ (create a free online account)
  • ESRI: https://www.esri.com/en-us/home (create a free online account)

    • During the semester, we will create accounts for these sites on an as-needed basis. In the mean-time, feel free to explore their websites.
