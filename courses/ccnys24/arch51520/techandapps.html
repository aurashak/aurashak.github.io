Technology and Apps

This course will rely heavily on digital technology. All software required for this course will be freely accessible, or accessible at the campus computer lab. In terms of hardware, for most exercises, a tablet computer should be sufficient, although accessing some programs will require a laptop or desktop coomputer, which can either be your own personal computer or one accessed at the computer lab. Specific programs will be using include:

  • Leaflet maps: https://leafletjs.com/
  • CesiumJS: maps (requires you to create an account): https://cesium.com/platform/cesiumjs/
  • Google apps: creating a Google account will give you access to many of the applications we will use this semester
  • QGIS: https://qgis.org/de/site/ (reqiures software installation on laptop or desktop)
  • Github.com: https://github.com/ (create an account)
  • Open Street Maps: https://www.openstreetmap.org/
  • Mapbox: https://www.mapbox.com/
  • Unreal Engine (OPTIONAL): requires software installation and significant processing power

    • During the semester, we will create accounts for these sites on an as-needed basis. In the mean-time, feel free to explore their websites.
