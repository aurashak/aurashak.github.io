Assignment #2, Part 1: Map your indigenous area(s)
Due: February 13th


Step 1: Create the map:
  • A. Visit https://geojson.io

  • B. Use the tools to draw a boundary around the areas in the world that you consider indigenous to your family.
    • There is no hard definition of what "indigenous" means. Use our class discussion, the resources we reviewed in class, readings, and your own online research to define indigeneity for yourself.
    • Consider speaking with your elders, if possible, to find out detials.
      • Feel free to choose more than one area
    • large and/or small territories based on your definition and affiliation.
    • If you do not know or cannot map your indigenous areas, map one of the places you identified in assignment 1.



  • C.The webpage will produce a list of coordinates in the right column. Copy the coordinates by pushing this button.


  • D. Save the file with the .geojson extension (ex. kabul.geojson).



  • E. Rename the file and move it to the correct location.
