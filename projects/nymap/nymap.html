About
Infrastructure in New York City is contanstly shaping our environment by emitting toxins, posing a disaster threat, polluting the air and water, and more. Networks of censors around the city, in sataellites, and with other reemote sensing, are generating data that allows for tracking and research of environmental conditions. This map seeks to identify key infrastructure, social conditions, and to integrate evolving datasets on the environment.
