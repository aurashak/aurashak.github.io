The unit for carbon emissions in EDGAR is indeed metric tons of carbon dioxide equivalent (MtCO2e) per year. This unit encompasses not only carbon dioxide (CO2) emissions but also converts other greenhouse gases like methane (CH4) and nitrous oxide (N2O) into their equivalent amount of CO2 based on their global warming potential.
