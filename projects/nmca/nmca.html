NMCA (Northern Manhattan Climate Action Plan)
Date: 2017
The NMCA is a comprehensive vision for urban development in Upper Manhattan. The plan was designed by Aurash Khawarzad and Mateo Fernandez-Muro. This planning project was led by WE ACT for Environmental Justice and included participation from many others.
