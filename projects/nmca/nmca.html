NMCA (Northern Manhattan Climate Action Plan)
The NMCA is a comprehensive vision for urban development in Upper Manhattan. The plan was designed by Aurash Khawarzad and Mateo Fernandez-Muro. The content and public participation was led by local organization WE ACT for Envronmental Justice and included participation from many others.
