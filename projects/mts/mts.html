135th Streeet Marine Transfer Station
This map investigates the infrastructure along the West Harlem waterfront in New York City. This area includes a concentration of waste, energy, transportation, and other infrastructure. When completed, this model will be used to understand the long-term impacts that the infrastructure has had on the natural environment and communities of West Harlem. See here for map documentation.
